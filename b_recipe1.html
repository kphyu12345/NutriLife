Yogurt Parfait



Ingredients

Nutrition

  • ≈180 calories
  • 12g protein
  • 4g fiber

Instructions

  1. Start by slicing the banana into thin , even rounds.
  2. In a bowl or a cup , spoon 2-3 tablespoons of yogurt into the bottom , and gently spread it out to form a smooth base.
  3. Arrange a few slices of banana evenly on top of the yogurt. Sprinkle about ½ teaspoon ground sesame seeds over the banana slices.
  4. Repeat it again till filled up.
