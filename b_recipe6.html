Strawberry Chocolate Chips Oat Cookie



Ingredients

Nutrition

  • 160 calories
  • 2g protein
  • 2g fiber

Instructions

  1. Mash 2 bananas and add them into a bowl , along with peanut butter , oats and a teaspoon of baking powder. Mix it all up until it's combined
  2. Roll it into a bowl and press them down onto your baking tray.
  3. Cut strawberries into small chunks and push them onto the top
  4. Put dark chocolate chips onto the top as well
  5. Bake at 338 F for 15 minutes
