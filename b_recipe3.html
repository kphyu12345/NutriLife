Avocado Toast with Egg



Ingredients

Nutrition

  • ≈270 calories
  • 12g protein
  • 7g fiber

Instructions

  1. Boil a pot of water, gently add the eggs, and cook for 9–10 minutes. Transfer them to cold water to cool, then peel and slice into rounds.
  2. While the eggs are cooking, toast your bread slices to your preferred level of crispness. Use a toaster or grill pan for best results.
  3. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash it with a fork until smooth.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the toasted bread slices. Make sure to cover the surface all the way to the edges for full flavor in every bite.
  5. Arrange the boiled egg slices neatly on top of the avocado spread.
