Plain Yogurt and Seasonal Fruit Bowl



Ingredients

Nutrition

  • ≈200 calories
  • 15g protein
  • 4-5g fiber

Instructions

  1. Rinse all fruits thoroughly under cold water. Chop or slice the fruits into small, bite-sized pieces to make them easy to mix and eat.
  2. In a medium mixing bowl, add one cup of plain yogurt. You can use regular yogurt for a lighter texture or Greek yogurt for a creamier, more protein-rich option. Gently fold in the chopped fruits, mixing just enough to distribute them evenly throughout the yogurt without breaking them down too much.
  3. If you like a touch of sweetness, drizzle in some honey or maple syrup. For added crunch and nutrition.
